A shared reference of key terms used. Terms are defined here to ensure alignment between both teams. FMSI will flag terms they need defined and our team will populate the definitions.
Term
Definition
Policy Premium
The dollar amount charged for the insurance policy for the selected term. Typically reflects the policy's written premium (before or after discounts/fees depending on the data source) and may be shown as annual premium or the full term premium.
Next Renewal Date
The date the policy is scheduled to renew next (i.e., the start date of the next policy term). Commonly aligns with the current policy expiration date (or the effective date of the renewal term, depending on how your agency management system reports it).
Need a term defined?
Use the Contact Us button to request a new term, and our team will add the definition. Please allow time for our team to research and incorporate new definitions.
